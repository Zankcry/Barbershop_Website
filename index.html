Meet Charlie "The Barberian"

Meet Charlie Domingo, the passion and skill behind every cut and style at our salon. Known as "Charlie the Barberian," he has built a reputation for excellence and creativity as a leading barber in Pampanga.

Located conveniently in front of Holy Angel University (HAU) in Angeles City, our shop is a go-to spot for students and locals alike. We are known for our busy, energetic atmosphere and top-quality barbershop service. We thrive on direct feedback and our strong social media presence to constantly improve.

The Beginning

Charlie's Barber & Salon started with a simple yet powerful vision: to create a space where artistry meets precision. Located near Holy Angel University, what began as a passion project has grown into Angeles City's trusted spot for style, grooming, and modern hair color.

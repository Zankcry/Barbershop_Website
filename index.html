Meet Charlie "The Barberian"

Meet Charlie Domingo, the passion and skill behind every cut and style at our salon. Known as "Charlie the Barberian," he has built a reputation for excellence and creativity.

Located conveniently in front of Holy Angel University, our shop is a go-to spot for students and locals alike, known for its busy, energetic atmosphere and top-quality service. We thrive on direct feedback and our strong social media presence to constantly improve and deliver the styles you love.

The Beginning

Charlie's Barber & Salon started with a simple yet powerful vision: to create a space where artistry meets precision. Located near Holy Angel University, what began as a passion project has grown into Angeles City's trusted spot for style and grooming.

The "Barberian"

Led by Charlie Domingo, known as "The Barberian", we blend traditional barbering techniques with modern trends. We aren't just cutting hair; we are crafting identities, ensuring every client leaves looking sharp and feeling unstoppable.

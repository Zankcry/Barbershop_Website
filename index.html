Our Story

From a simple vision to becoming Angeles City's trusted barbershop

The Beginning

Charlie's Barber & Salon began with a vision to create a space where artistry meets precision. What started as a passion project has grown into one of Angeles City's most trusted barbershops, serving the community near Holy Angel University.

The Vision

Charlie Domingo, "The Barberian," combined traditional barbering skills with modern styling techniques. With years of experience and commitment to excellence, he built a reputation for delivering cuts that make a statement.

Our Community

Located in front of Holy Angel University, we've become a favorite destination for students, professionals, and families throughout Pampanga. Our vibrant atmosphere reflects our dedication to staying current while maintaining timeless quality.

What Sets Us Apart

Our commitment to continuous improvement and genuine passion for the craft. Every service is crafted with attention to detail and a focus on building confidence.
